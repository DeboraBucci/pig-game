WELCOME TO PIGGY GAME!

Easy Game Rules

GOAL POINTS : 20

If you get dice number one you'll lose half of your current points.

This game difficulty is recommended for children!

NORMAL Game Rules

GOAL POINTS: 50

If you get dice number one, you'll lose all of your current points.

Hard Game Rules

GOAL POINTS: 100

If you get dice number one, you'll lose all your current points, and half your total scores.

Good luck in this one!
